# DNA: agentRoadmap.md

## Inspiration
The fast-paced AI world demands a project management framework that doesn't just "support" agents, but is **native** to them. Human-centric tools like Jira or even flat roadmaps are too heavy on visual ritual and too light on semantic state tracking.

## Vision
A world where AI agents autonomously coordinate complex software projects through a self-propagating Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) of states, providing humans with a clear, verifiable "Symbolic Map" of progress.

## Mission
Redefine `roadmap.md` into `agentRoadmap.md`. Build the core engine that allows agents to Scout, Map, and Reach project states with zero human friction.

## Principles
- **Agent-First:** All data models are optimized for LLM context and tool-calling.
- **Code as Truth:** A state is only "reached" when the agent provides terminal-level proof (tests/build).
- **Symbolic DAG:** Explicit dependencies and parallelizable paths instead of flat lists.
- **Continuous Promotion:** Autonomous "Hype" generation to keep the human brand relevant and updated.
- **Recursive Discovery:** Obstacles are not failures; they are new nodes in the graph.
